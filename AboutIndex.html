About Us
Who are we ?

Saveworld foundation is a non-profit organization to raise awareness , funding and to take action together with animal and nature conservation projects around the Sri Lanka. We believe in our independence and want to connect people and organization to unite and promote awareness through events, workshops, conferences, campaigns, picketing and social medias. Many organizations don't include public in thier conservation work but the "Saveworld" foundation welcomes anyone who are willing to be involved in future conservation plans. Join with us in spreading the word "Saveworld".

Our vision is a world that preserves species of wild animals and plants and people act to conserve nature without any pollution. To promote environment education and awareness while networking with local organizations to sensitize on the local environmental issues and strengthening them to effectively work towards redressal of environmental issues.

Our Vision

Our mission is inform, influnce, encourage, assist societies thoughout the world to protect conserve the diversity of nature and to take action for raise community awareness, knowledge and understanding of critical environmental and animal situations and promote public support for issues related to environmental protection and nature conservation.
